Gameplay

Objectives:

Main objective of the game is to make the person pass all the hurdles which are coming in his way and then pass all the difficulties which are added in game environment. The objective is to achieve as many points as possible by repeatedly interact the game over trigger. User will also entertain himself in free time by playing this game.

Game Progressions:

When the player reaches 700 points, the game switches from black graphics on white the level of the game will be changed to white graphics on black. Reaching a score of 900 will switch the color scheme back to day light, and the switch back and forth will occur at further subsequent milestones. For example, 1400 points is night mode, and 1600 points is day.

Game-GUI:

Tapping the dinosaur (in Android or iOS) or pressing space (on desktop) launches the game in which the player controls a running dinosaur by tapping the screen (in Android or iOS) or pressing space (on desktop) to avoid obstacles, including cacti and pterodactyls.
